Postcss, PX to REM

Lee Murray, 24 August 2017

Just a short post today, when I’m designing in the browser I like to work in px as it’s easier for my brain to calculate spacing but I like the flexibility rem units bring to layouts, now you can have the best of both worlds.

Postcss-pxtorem to the rescue!

Have a look at the repo at https://github.com/cuth/postcss-pxtorem, pxtorem will let you develop your designs in px but they will get converted to rem units automatically in your final output.

Postcss-pxtorem is a plugin for Postcss its been beautifully described as “a Swiss army knife for CSS production”, here’s some more info on it.
